About Us

  • This is your Home, This is your Church, This is your Family.

    Welcome to JCSGO Kasiglahan, a vibrant community of faith dedicated to serving and worshiping our Lord. Our church is more than just a place of worship; it's a family where you can find love, support, and spiritual growth.

     Our Mission Statement
      To fulfil the vision of seeking ang saving the lost, discipling them until they are established and training them for future leadership.

     Our Beliefs
      We are a congregation rooted in Christian principles, guided by the teachings of Jesus Christ. The Bible as the Word of God, and the importance of prayer and community in our spiritual journey.

     Our Worship Services
      We offer a variety of worship services to accommodate different schedules and preferences, from traditional hymns to contemporary praise and worship. Come as you are, and feel the presence of God in our worship.

     Our Ministries
      JCSGO Kasiglahan is more than just a church; it's a place where you can actively participate in various ministries to serve the community. We have ministries for children, youth, adults, and outreach programs, providing opportunities for everyone to get involved and make a difference.
